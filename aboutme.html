About Me

Hello, I'm David Eshuis! I'm passionate about all things game development and digital media creation. I've developed and released mulitple games on various platforms including Google Play, Appstore, and Steam. I've also worked with clients to deliver digital art commissions, including Twitch profile themes and podcast thumbnail images. I have over 5 years of experience programming with C# in Unity, as well as asset creation including 3D modeling, texturing, shaders, music, and sound effect creation. My goal is to dive deeper into the game development industry and one day start my own indie game studio. I've already released my first commercial game project, Tank Quest, on Steam, so I am familiar with key aspects of delivering a finished game product.

In my spare time I enjoy hiking, traditional art, playing video games, and watching movies with my friends!
